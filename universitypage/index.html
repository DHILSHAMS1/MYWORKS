The University of Calicut, also known as Calicut University, is a state-run public university headquartered at Tenhipalam in Malappuram district of the state of Kerala, India. Established in 1968, it is the first university to be set up in northern Kerala

UG

The main university campus is located at Tenhipalam, 24 km (15 mi) south of the city of Calicut and about 30 km (19 mi) from city of Malappuram. Most of the teaching and research departments, the Office of the Vice-Chancellor, and the Administration Block are situated on the main campus.

PG

The university was established through a Kerala government plan bifurcating the Kerala University. As per the plan, the four post-graduate departments of the Kerala University operating in Calicut were annexed to the new university

Diploma

The university also has two off-campus centres, one at Thrissur and the other, specially meant for tribal empowerment, located in the Western Ghat area of the state, Wayanad. Besides these, there are self-financing centres located in different parts of the area of jurisdiction.

Our Facilities

Calicut University Facilities - The university gives utmost importance to students' comfort. University of Calicut (CU) campus has all the facilities to offer a secure and pleasant ambience to admitted students. Calicut University campus facilities include library, computer labs, gym, wifi, guest room, auditorium, convenient store etc. For accommodation, outstation students can avail University of Calicut hostel facilities. The varsity has five cafeterias which offer healthy food at a reasonable price. For extracurricular activities students can use Calicut University sports facilities. The university has provision for various outdoor and indoor sports.
